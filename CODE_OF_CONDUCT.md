# Code Of Conduct

## Our Commitment
Contributors and maintainers are expected to make participation respectful, constructive, and professional for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful in discussions and reviews.
- Focus feedback on code and decisions, not people.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions when uncertain.
- Share actionable, reproducible technical context.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or intimidation.
- Deliberate disclosure of private or sensitive information.
- Repeated disruptive behavior in issues, PRs, or discussions.

## Enforcement
Maintainers are responsible for enforcing this policy and may:
- request behavior changes
- remove inappropriate content
- limit or revoke participation rights for severe/repeated violations

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report privately to maintainers. Include relevant links and context for investigation.
